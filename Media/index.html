Download & Setup

  1. Scan QR: Use your phone camera to scan the QR code and click the GitHub link.
  2. Download: Click the link to download the AR_Wetlands.apk file. Open it when finished.
  3. Permissions: Open "Begin Experience" and click Allow for camera access.
  4. Scan Marker: Approach the table. Scan the printed marker from top-down, then slowly pull back.
  5. Start: The scene will appear. You are ready to explore!

How It Works

  • Detection: The app uses your camera to lock onto the marker and spawn the wetlands scene.
  • Exploration: Walk around the table to see the scene from different angles.
  • Audio (🔊): Face the speaker icon directly and tap it to hear narration. Tap again to stop.

Troubleshooting

Scene Misaligned?
If the scene looks wrong or duplicated:
  1. Close the app completely.
  2. Reopen and scan at a slight angle.
  3. Slowly back away to let it spawn.

Note: This is a known limitation of the Unity AR platform.
